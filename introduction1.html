About BART LAB

Biomedical Engineering and Robotics Technology Laboratory, Mahidol University

Introduction

At BART LAB, we are passionate about bridging the gap between humans—especially surgeons—and intelligent machines such as computers and robots. Our laboratory is a thriving hub where innovation, technology, and human expertise converge to create groundbreaking solutions in biomedical engineering.

We believe that the synergy between human intuition and robotic precision can unlock new possibilities in healthcare, making treatments safer, more effective, and accessible to all. Our diverse research portfolio spans from cutting-edge robot-assisted surgery and advanced rehabilitation engineering to the development of smart hospital logistics systems.

By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and encouraging creative problem-solving, BART LAB is committed to shaping the future of medicine and engineering. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey as we work together to revolutionize healthcare practices and improve lives through visionary research and innovative partnerships.

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Jackrit Suthakorn

Founding Executive Director of BART LAB

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Jackrit Suthakorn — a visionary pioneer in biomedical and surgical robotics — has dedicated his career to advancing Thailand’s medical innovation through intelligent automation and human-machine synergy. With a Ph.D. in Robotics from Johns Hopkins University (USA), Dr. Jackrit has continually bridged research and real-world impact, cultivating interdisciplinary collaboration that shapes the next generation of medical technologies.His leadership and relentless pursuit of excellence have inspired countless students and researchers to join the frontier of biomedical engineering, where compassion meets precision and innovation serves humanity.

Ph.D. in Robotics, Johns Hopkins University, USA
Specialization: Surgical Robotics, Haptic Systems, and Rehabilitation Engineering

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading research laboratory in biomedical engineering and robotics technology, advancing innovation and improving human quality of life through intelligent machines.

Mission

To develop cutting-edge robotic systems, foster academic and industrial collaborations, and cultivate the next generation of biomedical engineers with strong ethics and creativity.
