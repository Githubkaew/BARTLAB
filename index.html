About BART LAB

BART LAB (Biomedical Engineering, Assistive Robotics and Rehabilitation Technology Laboratory) is a leading research center focusing on the development of robotics and medical technologies to enhance human health and quality of life. We collaborate with international institutions and industries to drive innovation and create real-world impacts.

Robotics & Automation

Design and development of assistive and surgical robots for advanced healthcare solutions.

Artificial Intelligence

Applying AI and machine learning in biomedical signal processing and intelligent systems.

Rehabilitation Technology

Creating assistive devices and rehabilitation robots to improve patient recovery outcomes.
