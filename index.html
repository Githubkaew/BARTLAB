Welcome to Our BART LAB

At BART LAB, we are passionate about bridging the gap between humans-especially surgeons-and intelligent machines such as computers and robots. Our laboratory is a thriving hub where innovation, technology, and human expertise converge to create groundbreaking solutions in biomedical engineering. We believe that the synergy between human intuition and robotic precision can unlock new possibilities in healthcare, making treatments safer, more effective, and accessible to all. Our diverse research portfolio spans from cutting-edge robot-assisted surgery and advanced rehabilitation engineering to the development of smart hospital logistics systems. By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and encouraging creative problem-solving, BART LAB is committed to shaping the future of medicine and engineering. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey as we work together to revolutionize healthcare practices and improve lives through visionary research and innovative partnerships
